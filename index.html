Advancing Precision Medicine

A new paradigm for Model-Informed Drug Discovery and Development (MIDD) leveraging the synergistic integration of Agentic AI, computational modeling, and mechanistic science.

The Foundation: PBPK Modeling

Physiologically Based Pharmacokinetic (PBPK) models create a virtual representation of an organism by linking major organs through the circulatory system.

A Mechanistic View of the Body

Each organ is a compartment defined by physiological parameters like volume and blood flow. This bottom-up approach enables prediction of drug ADME.

Simulating Drug Disposition

Watch how a drug distributes throughout the virtual organism, with concentrations changing in different organs over time.

Specialized Systems

Our framework enhances PBPK by integrating specialized reproductive and thyroid tissues for enhanced precision.

The Challenge: Biological Complexity

Traditional PBPK models struggle with the immense variability between and within individuals.

Inter-Individual Variability

Genetic differences, anatomical variations, age, and lifestyle factors create unique pharmacokinetic profiles.

Intra-Individual Variability

Dynamic physiological changes over time alter how drugs are processed in the body.

Data Scarcity

Limited comprehensive data for specific tissues creates modeling bottlenecks.
